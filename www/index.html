reference.data.gov.uk

This server contains reference data from the UK Government.

  • Open Government Licence URI /id/open-government-licence
  • Time Intervals Ontology
    • Vocabulary: /def/intervals
    • URIs eg: /id/year/2017
    • Docs: https://github.com/epimorphics/IntervalServer/blob/master/interval-uris.md
    • Source: http://source.data.gov.uk/def/reference/
    • Service created by Epimorphics and run by GDS.
  • Department URIs
    • e.g. /id/department/co
    • This data was sourced from the organogram data
    • URLs are now redirected to the Web Archive at The National Archives
    • NB These URIs were frozen on 3/10/16 and are now considered deprecated (as of 15/12/16)
  • Government Ontology
    • Vocabulary: /def/central-government
    • NB This not being maintained (since approx. 2010)
    • Source: http://source.data.gov.uk/def/reference/

This domain was was hosted afresh on 15th December 2016 to maintain continuity for the most important URIs/URLs. Maintained by GDS.
